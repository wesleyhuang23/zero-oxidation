ANTI-OXIDANTS

What are Anti-Oxidants?

Anti-oxidants are molecules that inhibit the oxidation of other molecules. It does this by providing the free radical molecule with one of its own electrons rendering it harmless. This works with the anti-oxidants our bodies make and the ones we take in our diet

What is ORAC?

ORAC is an acronym for "Oxygen Radical Absorbtion Capacity". It is the value given to certain foods and supplments indicating the absorption potential of free radicals.
