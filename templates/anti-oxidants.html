ANTI-OXIDANTS

What are Anti-Oxidants?

Anti-oxidants are molecules that inhibit the oxidation of other molecules. It does this by providing the free radical molecule with one of its own electrons rendering it harmless. This works with the anti-oxidants our bodies make and the ones we take in our diet

What is ORAC?

"A good way to measure the antioxidant activity of foods including juices is by means of the ORAC point system. ORAC stands for Oxygen Radical Absorption Capacity, a measure of the scavenging capacity of antioxidants against the peroxyl radical."

-Dr. Joel Wallch N.D and Dr. Ma Lan M.D
